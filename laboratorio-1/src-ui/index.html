Hollywood Park:
Tech Meets Urban Innovation

12-07-2025

cisco

Description

Hollywood Park partnered with Cisco to create a converged, wired Wi-Fi network and modern data center infrastructure for SoFi Stadium and the surrounding city-scale development. Hollywood Park uses the networking services of a modern city with a stadium, offices, condos, restaurants, a hotel, retail stores, a movie theater, and more

Challenge

  • Lower operational costs and provide smart resource management through automating operational tasks
  • Further sustainability efforts through a small hardware footprint and reduction in power usage
  • Provide centralized network management for real-time monitoring
  • Have ultra-reliable infrastructure to ensure uninterrupted operations for critical systems, including security, ticketing, point of sale, and broadcasting
